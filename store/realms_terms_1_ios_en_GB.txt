Realms Plus is a subscription for content and Realms personal server to be used with supported versions of Minecraft (game sold separately) on iOS and other supported platforms on which you own the game (Minecraft Java Edition not supported); an active subscription is required to use Realms Plus content. Content varies over time and may not be available in all countries. After the free trial period, subscription continues to be charged at the then-current regular price (subject to change) plus applicable taxes unless cancelled. Cancel anytime by accessing your iTunes Account settings. If your subscription terminates or a content item is removed from the catalogue, you must reactivate your subscription or buy the content separately to continue using it. If your subscription is inactive for more than 18 months, your Realms data will be deleted from the server. ISP fees may apply. Realms Plus discounts cannot be combined with other offers and are not redeemable for cash; Requirements, features, online services, or subscription subject to change and/or retirement.
